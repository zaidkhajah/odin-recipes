Air Fryer Lemon Garlic Parmesan Chicken Recipe

These simple lemon garlic Parmesan chicken thighs are cooked in the air fryer. Seasoned with lemon zest, garlic, and oregano and coated in Parmesan cheese they come out juicy and tender.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds skinless boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • ½ cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Coat the chicken generously with cooking spray.
  4. Lightly coat air fryer basket with cooking spray. Place chicken in prepared basket (cook in batches, if needed).
  5. Air fry 12 minutes or until an instant-read thermometer inserted in the center reads 170 degrees F (77 degrees C).
